Our Clients

Today, we have used the knowledge we’ve gathered from supporting various clients in industries such as Healthcare, Real Estate, Government, Hospitality, Construction, etc.. – and applied our knowledge to solve difficult problems in the Software-as-a-Service space.

You use Microsoft Office 365, Google Workspace (formerly GSuite) among others. They have geo-redundant storage facilities. They are protecting my data, or so you think.

The big cloud app publishers are focused on making sure the software they sell works without interruptions or problems. Operational infrastructure is their primary concern. They want to be sure that when a server fails, there is an instantaneous failover available. Their customers will not miss a beat. They are not concerned with storing or protecting your data. It’s explicitly stated in the Terms of Service that users are responsible for their data.
